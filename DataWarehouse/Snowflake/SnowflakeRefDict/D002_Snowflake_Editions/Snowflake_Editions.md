### Snowflake Pricing

- We are charged for Compute and Storage.
- **Compute** - Active Warehouse per hour.
- **Storage** - Average Storage used per month.

For Storage there are 2 types:

1. On Demand Storage
2. Capacity Storage

Total Amount Of Storage in **Usage** consists of:

- Database
- Stage
- Fail Safe
